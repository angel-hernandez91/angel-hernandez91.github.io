About Nibbler

My name is Dr. Nibbles Nibbler Nibblstein. I hold PhD's in Neurobiology, Computer Science, Physics, Mathematics, and Chicano Studies. Several decades as go I wrote the infamous 1973 paper the Sex of a Fly, which brought the Life Sciences into the mainstream. But not all that glittered was gold; due to allegations of falsified data I was exiled from the scientific community and my work was lost to the sands of time.

Many people believed that I falsified data to rise to the top of my field so I could live a life of luxury. A life of golden showers, fast cars, and even faster, hairless men. I was constantly harassed by the press, and my closest friends and lover, Carlito, abandoned me. It's been a wild ride. I developed a severe addiction to cocaine, robbed several liquor stores, and ended up in jail where I thought I would waste away until Angel broke me out by paying off the guards.

Academic research is a thing of the past for me. These days I spend my time napping, being tormented by that orange bastard Fry, rubbing my face on Angel's beard, and trying to get as many belly pats as possible. I never spoke out back then because I was afraid I would look like a lunatic. But now, in the days of Russian hacking, crooked politicians, and a rejected Cheeto for a President, this doesn't seem so crazy after all.

I was set up to fail by Big Oil because I discovered a way to turn Mexican tears into fuel using bromoacetone, quarks, and fruit fly penises using an Advanced Quantum Deep Learning AI Model. This would've completed disolved our dependence on oil, and would've ushered in an era where Mexicans were venerated, respected, and accepted into this country. It was too radical for the time, and possibly even still to radical now. But I urge you people of the internet, rise up and fight against Exxon Mobil and the rest of the energy giants. Ask them, "Why was Dr. Nibbles set up? Why was Nib Nib's work destroyed?". Demand the true, for it shall set us all free.

About Fry

My name is Fry and I'm a sneaky, orange bastard. I am deeply fascinated by water, ice cubes, and the dirt the humans keep their plants in. I also enjoy leaves, but the humans don't like when I eat them. I try to eat their food, but they don't always give me any. They say it's bad for me and that I'll die, but they seem okay.

I like to hang out in windows and chirp at the squirrels. All of my attempts to communicate with them have failed. I know that if I could just make it past the force field and into the outside I could finally reach them and gain access to their secrets.

I have a girlfriend named Shelly. We go out sometimes, but I always have to carry her since she can walk. Although sometimes she seems to magically transport into different rooms. I have come to the conclusion that she has magic powers that only work when I am not present. I hope one day she can show me how to teleport because it would really help me get onto the high selves where humans store their food.

I really love Nibbler even though he hated me in the beginning. I tried so hard to be his friend by constantly pouncing on him, bitting his tail, and licking his face. I don't know what that didn't work right away. I was being so friendly! But in the end it all worked out. He did slap me a few times, but we moved passed that. Now we zoomy together, cuddle, eat, and boop. I learned the he's an old man and sometimes I have to take it slow. And I should never, ever bring up the Sex of a Fly. He goes on forever about government conspiracies and the justs gets made and hides under the bed.
